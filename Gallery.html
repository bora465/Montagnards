Montagnard Flag



This flag unites all the MTDs. Green for the mountains and forests of the Highlands. White for peace and honesty. Red represents the blood and struggle that the MTDs have gone through and the circle of gold place emphasis on justice and friendship. The elephant represents the people, gentle but powerful.

Miss Universe Vietnam



H'Hen Nie born on May 15 1992, Buon Ma Thuot, Vietnam, won Miss Universe Vietnam 2018 and is the first Montagnard to ever win the title. She represented Vietnam at Miss Universe 2018 and finished in the Top 5.

Population



According to the 1971 census, there was a population of approximately one million and today, there are about four million Montagnards. Today, outside of the Highlands, majority of Montagnard lives in North Carolina. Major cities include Greensboro, Raleigh, and charlotte, but however, Montagnards can settle anywhere other than those cities and the Highlands.

Behind the Names



You can easily tell if a person is Montagnard by how their name is spelled. Females would have and H' in front of their names and males would have a Y' in the front. But not all MTDs have it in their names, but you can particularly look at their last names.

    Common Last Name includes:
  • Siu, Ksor, Puih, Thinh, Ayun, Hmok, Eban
  • Rahlan, Rcom, Rocham, R(o)mah, Rolan
  • Nie, Thi, Adrong, Mlo, Kpa ...
